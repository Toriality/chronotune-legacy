What is Chronotune?

Music has the power to transport us through time and space, and with Chronotune, you can put your knowledge of music history to the test.

The game's concept is simple! Guess the year that a randomly selected song was released. With each correct guess, you earn points, and in the end of the match, you'll be able to see your score.

How to Play Chronotune?

Playing Chronotune is a breeze! Just follow these steps:

  • Start the game by clicking on the "Start Game" button.
  • A random song will be loaded.
  • Click on the colorful box to start playing the song.
  • Use your knowledge to guess the year the song was released. Use the slider on the bottom of the screen to slide through the years.
  • Click on the "Confirm" button to reveal the answer.
  • Click on the "Next song" button to advance to the next song.
  • After 5 matches, you will be able to see your final score and your high score.

The Music in Chronotune

One of the unique aspects of Chronotune is its broad music selection. The game features songs from the Spotify's database, meaning you'll get millions of different songs each time you play Chronotune!

We build a special algorithm for Chronotune that filters off most of the invalid songs like AudioBooks and Podcasts, that way, you'll get the best experience possible while playing.

The Educational Value of Chronotune

Chronotune is not just a game; it's also an educational experience. By playing Chronotune, you can learn about the history of music and how different genres have evolved over time. With songs from different eras and styles, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of popular (and not so popular) music history.

Chronotune is an excellent way to discover new music. You might come across a song you've never heard before that you absolutely love. And with songs from different genres, you might discover a new favorite artist or band. Variety and diversity is our top priority!
